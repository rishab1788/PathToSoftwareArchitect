"Think," "Should," and "Disagree" are commonly used in business settings to express opinions, recommendations, and differing viewpoints. To convey these messages more professionally and politely, consider using softer alternatives. For example, instead of "Think," use "I believe" or "I feel." Instead of "Should," use "You might want to" or "It could be helpful to.”
