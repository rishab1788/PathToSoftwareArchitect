Asking questions with W-Words
Open-ended questions usually start with one of the W-Words below:

What
How
Which
Why
Where
When
Who
Now, try pairing a safe small talk topic with a W-Question Word to create an interesting question for small talk. Here are some examples: 

Weather: "How do you feel about today's weather?"
Travel: "Where did you last go on holiday?"
Food: "What's your favorite meal from your country?"
Hobbies: "When did you start liking [a hobby, like painting]?"
News: "How do you feel about the new space news?"
